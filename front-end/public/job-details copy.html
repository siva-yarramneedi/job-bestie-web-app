Instructions to Follow Before Applying for a Job

1. Read the Job Description
Carefully go through the job description to understand the role, responsibilities, and required skills.
2. Check Your Skills
Compare the required skills with your own. Identify your strengths and areas for improvement.
3. Verify Eligibility Criteria
Ensure you meet the required qualifications, experience, and any other criteria mentioned in the job posting.
4. Update Your Resume & Cover Letter
Customize your resume and cover letter to highlight relevant skills and experiences that match the job.
